The 1-line accessibility widget that adapts your site to your visitors' needs without touching a single component.

== Description ==

Inkluyo Accessibility is an ultra-lightweight widget that instantly allows your visitors to adapt your website to their specific needs (such as Dyslexia, Low Vision, or Reduced Mobility). 

Instead of rewriting your entire theme, Inkluyo loads a discreet button on your site. When users click it, they can toggle accessibility features. 

Major Features Include:
* **Dyslexia Mode**: Swaps fonts to highly legible typefaces.
* **High Contrast**: Inverts colors intelligently.
* **Calm Mode**: Pauses all animations.
* **Big Cursor**: Enlarges the cursor for better visibility.
* Custom Color and Position matching your brand.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `inkluyo-accessibility` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to Settings -> Inkluyo to configure your widget's color, position, and language.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Will this hurt my page load speed? =
No. The script is tiny (~7kb) and loads asynchronously (deferred). Your Lighthouse scores will not be impacted.
